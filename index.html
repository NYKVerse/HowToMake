STEP 1

Find an instrument!

You need an instrument that you can play to be able to make music. It's not required to use music instruments. You can use something else that can make sound like a table! You just need instruments to make melodies.

STEP 2

Make a random melody or chords!

If you don't know what melody or chord is, you can't make music 🧍‍♂️. Learn some basic first and make random melodies! it helps you making your music!

STEP 3

Think of something that you want to make!

Anything can be music and songs! Most of music are made out of the creator's feelings, but it can be anything you want! Friendships, Memories, or about a tea you just drank in the morning! (or this blue book you saw at the left of the screen)

STEP 4

Make Beats!

Beats are cool! You can make beats with some music loop softwares! Fun fact, Beats sometimes generate a feeling to listener about what the creator feel when making music. If it has a fast beat, means it's fun. If it's a very fast beats, the creator is drowning or trying to run away from things they currently had. If it's slow, means the creator's sad.

STEP 5

Finally, Write Songs with Rhymes!

What is "Rhymes?". Rhymes is like words that has same pronouncements at the ending (example: You, Clue, Blue, Flew, Blew. They are rhymes!)

With Rhymes, you can make a cool perfect song!
